What I am looking to solve for: A catastrophe is ultimately a cascade of failures, so that technical debt exacerbates these concerns and adds to the fragility of the system. Done Done means that a deliberate effort is made to ensure that specification of the system is complete and that all the expectations implied are reliably delivered.
